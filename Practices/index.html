Practice Information

Why Do Patients Love Our Practice?

We Care... We respect your time and seat you with minimum wait in the reception room. Dr. Surana treats only one patient at a time, and hence can deliver the best dental care by focusing only on You while you are being treated.

We provide Comfort... You'll be amazed how easy your visit can be! Comfortable chairs, blankets, pillows, wireless headphones, wifi and TV to watch are just a few of the many things we provide our patients to ensure your comfort.

We have Advanced Technology. .. We have incorporated the latest technology to help improve the treatments given to you.

- Digital X-Rays (Radiation Exposure Reduced by 80%)

- In-Office Whitening

- Ultrasonic Scalers

- Invisialign

- Removal of Mercury Fillings

We're Affordable... Getting the smile you've always wanted has never been easier with our many payment options. We accept Visa, MC, and Discover credit cards. We are also pleased to offer Care Credit to those who qualify, so that interest-free monthly payments can be made if needed.

For those of you who are fortunate to have dental insurance we will be more than happy to submit all insurance forms for you and help you recover the most from your benefits. We will do everything we can to help you afford the treatment you need and want.
