Laguna Niguel Dentist - Dr. Rachna Surana

Dr. Rachna Surana earned her Bachelor's of Dental Surgery from India in 2002 and graduated with seven GOLD medals and was also in Dean's list. She then received her California License (DDS) in the US from UOP, San Francisco CA.

She has been practicing dentistry for more than 10 years and has earned a reputation as a conscientious, skilled and compassionate dentist. Through her extensive training in all areas of dentistry and with thousands of hours of continuing dental education , she is able to give her patients the highest standard of care and treats her patients, as she would her own family.

When not practicing her passion of dentistry , Dr. Surana spends most of her time with her husband and their beautiful daughters! She loves doing yoga, hiking and cooking different vegetarian cuisines. She is also actively involved in community and volunteer works

Welcome to Our Website. We believe dentistry is not just a science of teeth...it's an art with service.

Dr. Rachna Surana
